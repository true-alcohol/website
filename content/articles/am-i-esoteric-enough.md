To ask whether one is *esoteric enough* is already to have misunderstood the
esoteric, which is never a quantity. The secret is not information withheld; it is
a relation to a threshold. One is not more or less initiated. One is either at the
door or has forgotten there was a door.

> To forget the ungraspable corruption that reigns in the heart is the black,
> dead sun.
